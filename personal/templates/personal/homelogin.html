    Why?

    By using recycled materials we save on energy consumption,
    which keeps production costs down.


    What?

    The following items should not be placed into your recycling bin:
    Any glass contaminated with stones, dirt, and food waste. Ceramics,
    such as dishware, ovenware, and decorative items.
    Heat-resistant glass, such as Pyrex. Mixed colors of broken glass.
    Mirror or window glass. Metal or plastic caps and lids. Crystal.


    How?

    What should we do with our old electronics?

    First, we should never throw e-waste in the trash!

    1. Pass them on for reuse.
    2. Recycle them.
    3. Find a good e-waste recycler.
    4. Staples stores.
    5. Best Buy stores.
    6. Do a cell phone recycling drive and fundraiser in your school.
    7. Learn to fix broken gadgets yourself.
